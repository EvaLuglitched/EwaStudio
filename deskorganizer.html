STUDIO DESK ORGANIZER

INTRO

An elegant fusion of functionality and modern design. This studio desk organizer modernizes your workstation with a minimalistic design, crafted from sleek acrylic board and durable plastic. This organizer redefines desk aesthetics while offering practical storage solutions for people who work in studios. While solving all your needs, it serves as a piece of decoration aligning with your working environment. This desk organizer comes with portable storage boxes in different sizes that maximize storage space without taking up too much space in your working area, along with considerate storage options for your personal belongings.

USER INTERVIEW

ANONYMOUS INDUSTRIAL DESIGN STUDENT

Age: 20

Primary Use of Product: Tool Storage, Phone Stand, Headphone Storage

" I prefer a clean workspace because I primarily spend my entire day in the studios. I have a lot of tools such as an exacto knife and a straight edge etc... I also put personal items like my headphones and water bottle in my workspace. They all lay on my table which is taking a lot of my workplace."

TARGET USER

Industrial designers and architects who spend most of their time working in studios, and have no time to organize their studio desks. Seeking an easy solution to organize their supplies and tools that wouldn't take up too much space in their working area.

PRODUCT VISUALIZATION

A modern minimalistic approach provides an elegant visual experience in the studio. Inspired by the interior design of Muji stores, the design took elements of wooden wall panels on interior decoration bringing a modern appearance to your desk. Three large-capacity baskets in different sizes allow you to store studio tools and supply accessories based on your needs. Very suitable for storing Execto knives, pens, scissors, and A4 paper.

All storage boxes are elevated from the task surface and can be slid around according to your needs; can be removed and replaced individually based on how you want to organize your workspace. Supporting structures are inspired by wooden wall panels on interior decoration bringing a modern appearance. They support the weight of storage boxes while providing more space underneath for rulers and longer tools to pass through.

The phone stand is built into the organizer and placed ergonomically on your eye level. Pull the phone stand up to place your phone on the desk organizer or put it in your bag and bring it wherever you go.

Place your headphones on the headphone rack away from foams and cardboard so it does not get all dusty when you work.
