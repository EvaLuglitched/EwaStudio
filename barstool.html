INTRO

This walnut barstool represents a harmonious blend of traditional Chinese architectural principles and contemporary design aesthetics. Drawing inspiration from the ancient joinery techniques found in temples and traditional buildings, the stool features carefully crafted mortise and tenon connections that eliminate the need for modern fasteners while ensuring structural integrity. odern elements like the leather upholstery and decorative silver dowels create a subtle contrast, bridging centuries of craftsmanship with present-day design sensibilities.
