An interactive birdhouse that promotes a bird-friendly community through collective public effort. Instead of leaving wildlife protection solely to environmental organizations, this design invites everyone to participate in supporting bird populations. With a simple tap on the birdhouse, users can provide food for local birds, making conservation accessible and engaging for all. SERVICE ECOSYSTEM

An interactive birdhouse that promotes a bird-friendly community through collective public effort. Instead of leaving wildlife protection solely to environmental organizations, this design invites everyone to participate in supporting bird populations. With a simple tap on the birdhouse, users can provide food for local birds, making conservation accessible and engaging for all. This small daily action creates meaningful impact, turning casual interactions into lasting habits while fostering a deeper connection between humans and birds.

PRODUCT DETAILS

The Beeper turns bird watching into an adventure. It alerts you when our bird friends are nearby. Just like playing Pokémon GO, but with real birds in your neighborhood. The beeper is small enough to fit in the pocket while displaying all necessary information efficiently.

AI SYSTEM & ECOLOGICAL DATABASE

Transforming bird behavior into actionable urban ecological insights through intelligent analysis and build a comprehensive urban bird ecosystem database.The process starts with collecting raw data through camera-equipped feeders that capture bird species, behavior patterns, and feeding habits, then uses machine learning for species identification and behavioral analysis, and finally outputs an ecological database for academic research, policy support, and urban planning.
