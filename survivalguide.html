INTRO

A discreet and playful executive toy designed as an interactive book for queer women in China. This coded survival guide cleverly conceals its true purpose through engaging puzzles and everyday objects, making it both an entertaining accessory and a meaningful communication tool. Each carefully crafted page contains hidden cultural references and community slang, accessible only to those who understand their significance. The guide's compact size allows it to blend seamlessly with daily accessories while creating a subtle yet powerful connection among its intended audience.
