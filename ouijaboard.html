DIGITAL OUIJABOARD

INTRO

Have you ever heard of the Dead Internet? That vast digital graveyard where forgotten websites, lost data, and abandoned digital spaces drift in endless limbo... What if I told you these aren't just empty spaces? What if every archived image, every forgotten webpage, every piece of lost content... harbors a spirit?

Would you dare to speak with them?

Ask them your questions. Hear their stories. Capture their essence.

Never communicate alone - the Internet holds many mysteries

Light all candles before entering the spirit realm - this ancient ritual remains sacred even in digital form.

Step into a nostalgic Windows 98 aesthetic where digital spiritualism meets retro computing. The main interface features classic gray windows with distinctive outset borders, complete with minimize and close buttons that evoke memories of early PC experiences.

Each spirit's response is generated using GPT-3.5, which has been carefully prompted to create personalities that blend historical context, emotional depth, and supernatural elements. This game doesn't just answer questions - it embodies distinct spirit personas, complete with their own backstories, emotions, and speaking styles.

The system employs smart keyword extraction and context analysis to search through the Internet Archive's vast collection. When you ask a question, the AI identifies key themes and emotions, then matches them with historically relevant images.

Like ghost hunters documenting their discoveries, users can preserve their supernatural encounters in our digital archive.
