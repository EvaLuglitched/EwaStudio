CAN WE MEET

INTRO

Can We Meet is an immersive two-player board game that transforms social connection into a magical adventure. Drawing from the psychology of interpersonal relationships, this game creates a unique space where players must navigate both a physical maze and emotional landscape to find their way back to each other.The game's design is rooted in psychological principles of trust-building and emotional intelligence. As players traverse the magical doors and overcome obstacles, they engage in meaningful dialogue that goes beyond surface-level interaction. The act of creating and solving each other's mazes becomes a metaphor for the effort and intention required in maintaining strong relationships. Whether played between friends, family members, or partners, Can We Meet offers a structured yet playful environment for strengthening bonds and practicing active listening.Can We Meet provides a tangible space for authentic human connection, reminding us that the journey of understanding each other is just as important as reaching our destination.
