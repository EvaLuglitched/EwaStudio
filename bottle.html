SMILE SHIELD INITIATIVE

INTRO

This project addresses dental health issues among individuals with low income. Neglect of dental health and financial constraints inflict physical and psychological harm, affecting daily life and overall physical well-being. Design highly accessible solutions to lower barriers and costs for low-income populations. Centered on early intervention as a core design principle, these solutions help low-income individuals address oral health issues promptly, thereby preventing delayed treatment. This project strategically integrating oral healthcare resources, advocating for the necessity of oral wellness, and promoting oral hygiene knowledge to empower individuals.

CONCEPT

Compared to other types of diseases, oral diseases affect approximately 3.5 billion people. The World Health Organization has identified these problems as urgent public health challenges requiring immediate attention. Disparities in income create gaps in nutrition and education, exacerbating oral disease incidence and treatment gaps. High out-of-pocket costs and low preventive care rates delay treatment, leading to worsening conditions and rising costs.

APP DESIGN

This dental health app is dedicated to promoting oral health equality through a systematic functional design that enhances users' health awareness and reduces medical expenses. The platform integrates five core modules: The Home section fosters daily preventive habits through a points-based medal system, community check-ins, and AR interactive games; the Diagnostics center offers one-stop services such as AI self-tests, online consultations, and offline bookings; the Study section strengthens health knowledge popularization via science articles, videos, and educational games; the Mall allows points redemption for medical services, charitable aid, and supplies, tangibly alleviating financial burdens; and the Mine personal center enables customized reminders and management. By combining preventive education, resource integration, and a points-based incentive system around the “prevention over treatment” philosophy, the app empowers users to achieve autonomous oral health management.
