INTRO

This Project is a study of the possibilities of office and studio chairs, inspired by the plastic chairs at my school. I explored numerous ways of elevating people's work experience, to present the most comfortable chair for working and relaxing. The Grand Lightness Inflatable Chair is not just a piece of furniture; it's an experience of comfort and versatility. Designed with a focus on modern aesthetics and functionality, this chair brings a sense of grandeur to any space. The chair transcends traditional seating. Its inflatable nature not only provides a unique and customizable sitting experience but also makes it incredibly lightweight and portable.

ANONYMOUS ILLUSTRATION STUDENT

Age: 21

Primary Use of Product: 6hrs Drawing Studios, Gaming

LOOK

" We not only have this in studios but in dorms as well. Not everyone needs a bright orange chair in their room, especially since all the rest of the pieces of furniture are wooden. Feels like a traffic cone squeezing my tight little ass."

"Our studio chairs are Insanely uncomfortable, they don't recline. We are Artists and we need to sit in those chairs for 8-hour studios and my back hurts a lot. I am the height of an average adult man but I'm still too tall for this chair."

RESEARCH SUMMARY

We see no deliberate thinking of design in the products we purchased as a design school. There is a lack of consideration for our students' health in the long term since the chairs we purchased are not convenient or comfortable.

MARKET ANALYSIS

According to the latest study, the global Ergonomic Office Chair market size was valued at USD 1.6 billion in 2023. With growing demand in the downstream market, the Ergonomic Office Chair is forecast to a readjusted size of USD 1.9 billion by 2030 with a CAGR of 5.3% during the review period. A great demand for office furniture is gradually growing due to the fact of increasing development of corporate hubs in the States, especially after the pandemic. EXPLODED VIEW

The chair consists of two inflatable cushions to provide back and neck support. Closely appresses to your body whether you are working in the studio or taking a break.

PRODUCT DETAILS

Unique furniture product, in line with your identity as an artist or designer. consists of trendy transparent color cushions that can be easily inflated with an electric pump. Unlike ordinary PVC, the cushions are made out of Thermoplastic Polyurethane which better supports your back due to its elastic nature. The chair can be assembled and disassembled in a few minutes, and then easily moved or stored.
