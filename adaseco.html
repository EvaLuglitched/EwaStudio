AUTONOMOUS DRIVING ASSISTANT SYSTEM

INTRO

A conceptual user interface for an autonomous driving assistant system. The interface incorporates a simulate urban enviroment map, car model, and a visualization of radar detection. The model demonstrates how the car would nevigate in different road senarios. This is a human-centered interface tailored to meet the unique need of each driver and vehicle

INFO

Role
Product Designer
Software
Solidworks, Rhino, Blender, Keyshot

After extensive research into current autonomous driving systems, I identified a significant gap between technical capabilities and user experience. I designed this concept UI/UX for an autonomous driving assistant that prioritizes the human element. My approach creates a human-centered interface that adapts to individual driver preferences and vehicle specifications, rather than forcing users to adapt to technology. I focused on creating an experience that enhances safety while providing intuitive visualization that doesn't distract from the road ahead.

PROJECT SCOPE

RESEARCH

Through this research, I've discovered there is a clear hierarchy of features with safety being the most critical priority. This systematic approach helped me establish design priorities that balance critical safety features with driver comfort and personalization options. By understanding what matters most to users.

COMPETITIVE ANALYSIS

I examined Waymo, Tesla, and Zoox across key dimensions to understand their strengths, weaknesses, convenience factors, and price points.More importantly, I discovered a critical opportunity space: while these systems focus primarily on technological capabilities, none has fully addressed the human-centered interface that puts users' unique needs first.

MOODBOARD

PROJECT VISUALIZATION & KEY FEATURES

The 3D visualization presents a clear and intuitive representation of the driving environment. I deliberately used low-poly models for surrounding vehicles to maintain essential information while minimizing visual complexity and cognitive load for the driver. I focused on creating intuitive visualizations of the autonomous system's technical operations such as collision avoidance, alerts, streetsigns, and parking assistant.
