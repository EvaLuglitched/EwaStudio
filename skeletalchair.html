Skeletal Chair

INTRO

This is a dinning chair that redefines the boundaries of lightweight furniture design. At just 5.3 pounds, this chair represents the absolute minimum in material usage while still delivering maximum comfort and stability. Engineered with precision-optimized aluminum and steel, the Skeletal Chair achieves its remarkable lightness without sacrificing ergonomics. This project was challenging because every component has been stripped to its essential form, yet the chair maintains all universal dimensions required for comfortable dining. The result is a visually striking piece that appears to float in space while providing substantive support.
